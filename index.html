How to use the Coffee Compass

First, brew a cup of coffee. Taste it and find a flavor on the compass. Desireable flavors are in the green center, while undesireable flavors are on the outside. If you detect an undesireable flavor, click on it, and a recommendation will be generated to improve your next brew. Continue this process until you are satisfied with your coffee.
